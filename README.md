# AURA AI 🚀

### Your AI Co-Founder for the Creator Economy

AURA AI helps creators grow smarter, earn better, and stay protected through AI-powered decision intelligence.

## Problem

Creators operate like businesses but lack professional tools for:

* Sponsorship valuation
* Contract analysis
* Scam detection
* Growth forecasting
* Brand partnership discovery

Many creators accept underpriced deals, sign risky contracts, and make business decisions without data-driven support.

## Solution

AURA AI acts as an AI Co-Founder by providing:

* Revenue Forecasting
* Burnout Risk Detection

### Protection Engine

* Contract Analysis
* Scam Detection
* Sponsorship Valuation

### Brand Match Engine

* Brand Compatibility Scoring
* Partnership Recommendations
